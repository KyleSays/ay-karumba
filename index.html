Our Story

Kevin, the Owner of AY ' Karumba decided to bring his passion, and love for food, to light. Born and raised in Baltimore City, Kevin was always given a home cooked as he watched his Grandma cook for his family. He learned to love and appreciate the taste in his Grandma's food. As he grew older, he decided to further his love for food and started his cooking journey and exploring the world of food. As his passion grew, he one day decided that "I can do this... I can open a café for people that want quality food and coffee to kickstart their busy day and start the morning on a positive note." And that's exactly what Kevin did. His main goal in opening AY ' Karumba was to bring quality food and coffee to the area and to bring a smile to his customers faces each and every day.
